Imperial Rulebook

1. Select Your Champions

Choose up to 3 cards on the table. These are your chosen outcomes. Click a selected card to unselect it.

2. Set the Stakes

Pick a wager amount (10, 20, or 50). This amount is charged per card selected.

3. The Triple Launch

Hold the [SPACE] bar or the LAUNCH button to build energy. Release when ready to drop 3 Imperial Orbs onto the table.

4. Claim Victory

If any orb lands on your chosen card, you win! The more orbs that land on your card, the higher the bounty!
